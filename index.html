What is CSGO-Buynds?

This web-application, CSGO-Buynds, is a simple tool to help gamers create and customize their own scripts which allow 1-button purchasing of weapons and equipment in the popular PC game Counter-Strike: Global Offensive. These scripts are often called "buy binds" or "buy scripts". CSGO-Buynds will help you escape that horrible radial buy menu forever!

Why use Buy Binds?

  • Competitive players often need to buy their weapons and equipment late, after the in-game leader is finished calling the strats
  • Casual servers usually only allow 5 seconds for buy freeze time
  • It is easier to learn & remember one key press as opposed to b42b52b54b65b64b63b13
  • Buy binds can help you drop weapons for teammates faster
  • Avoid getting spawn-killed in warmup
  • Throw extra decoy grenades as you leave spawn EVERY round

Create your own CS:GO Buy Binds!
